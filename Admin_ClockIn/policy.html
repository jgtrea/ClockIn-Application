Data Privacy Policy Statement

The Data Privacy Act of 2012 (Republic Act No. 10173) upholds the fundamental right to privacy of every individual by regulating the collection and processing of personal data in both government and private institutions. It mandates organizations to implement reasonable and appropriate organizational, physical, and technical measures to protect personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Under this law, data subjects have the right to be informed, to access, to object, to erasure or blocking, to damages, to file a complaint, to rectify, and to data portability. Institutions processing personal information are required to obtain the consent of data subjects, ensure lawful purpose, and maintain data accuracy and integrity throughout its life cycle.
